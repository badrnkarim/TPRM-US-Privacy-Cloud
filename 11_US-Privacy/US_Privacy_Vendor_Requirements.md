# US Privacy Vendor Requirements (Operational Baseline)
1) Purpose limitation (no secondary use)  
2) Minimization (field allowlist where possible)  
3) Retention schedule + verifiable deletion/return  
4) DSAR support obligations (contractual) + SLAs when required  
5) Subprocessor disclosure + flow-down + time-bound change notification  
6) Time-bound incident/breach notification commitment
