# TPRM Playbook (One Page)

## Objective
Make vendor decisions **risk-first** and **evidence-backed**:
**Impact + Exposure → Required Controls → Evidence → Residual Risk → Decision → Monitoring**

## Lifecycle
1) Intake (data type/classification, access paths, subprocessors)
2) Inherent risk scoring (0–100) → tier
3) Tiered due diligence + evidence request
4) Review evidence quality + control maturity
5) Residual risk scoring + decision
6) Contract/privacy requirements confirmed
7) Remediation (CAPA mindset) + verification
8) Ongoing monitoring + reassessment + offboarding

## Tiers + cadence
- **Critical:** annual + continuous monitoring
- **High:** annual
- **Medium:** every 24 months
- **Low:** every 36 months (or major change)

## Decision outcomes
- **Approve**
- **Approve with Conditions** (time-bound remediation + compensating controls)
- **Reject**
- **Exception/Risk Acceptance** (time-bound, documented approvals)

## Reassessment triggers
- New data type or higher classification
- New privileged/VPN/admin access
- Material change in subprocessors/toolchain
- Security incident or breach disclosure
- Contract renewal for High/Critical
