# Executive Summary
**Objective:** Reduce third-party risk through **risk-based controls** and **evidence-backed assurance**, with explicit attention to **US privacy obligations** and **cloud data protection**.

## Lifecycle
Intake → Inherent Risk → Tiering → Due Diligence → Evidence → Residual Risk → Decision → Remediation → Monitoring → Offboarding

## Decision outcomes
Approve | Approve with Conditions | Reject | Exception (time-bound, documented)

## Evidence discipline
Every control requirement is tied to an evidence item (EV-###) with reviewer notes and validity windows.
