# One-Page Executive Summary — US Privacy Cloud TPRM

**Purpose:** Reduce third-party risk by making vendor decisions **impact-driven**, **control-based**, and **evidence-backed**.

## What makes this US-grade
- Explicit US privacy expectations (CIPP/US-aligned): transparency, minimization, retention, consumer rights readiness, service provider obligations, breach preparedness
- Cloud data protection focus: shared responsibility, encryption + key management, access paths, logging, incident response
- Evidence discipline: every decision is supported by reviewable artifacts

## Lifecycle (end-to-end)
**Intake → Inherent Risk → Required Controls by Tier → Due Diligence → Evidence → Residual Risk → Decision → Remediation → Monitoring → Offboarding**

## Risk scoring
- **Inherent risk (0–100):** impact + exposure + data sensitivity + access + regulatory/privacy factors
- **Residual risk:** adjusted by control maturity + evidence confidence

## Decision outcomes
- **Approve**
- **Approve with conditions** (remediation deadline + compensating controls)
- **Reject** (unacceptable residual risk)
