# DSAR / Rights Readiness — Filled (Sanitized Example)

Vendor: DataPulse Analytics  
Tier: High  
Data: Large-scale identifiers (Confidential/Restricted)

## Capability commitments (required)
- Search: locate individual by email/ID across datasets
- Deletion: delete individual records + confirm completion (with subprocessor propagation)
- Export: provide structured export for contractual requests when required
- Correction: support correction or documented alternative (delete/reingest)

## Required SLAs (contractual)
- Acknowledge request: ≤ 5 business days
- Fulfill delete/export: ≤ 30 days (or contract-defined)
- Evidence: completion confirmation + ticket trail

## Evidence required
- Workflow description + sample ticket artifacts (sanitized)
- Data retention/deletion handling explanation for backups/derived datasets
