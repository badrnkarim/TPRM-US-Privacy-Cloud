# DSAR / Consumer Rights Vendor Readiness Checklist (Vendor)

> Purpose: Ensure vendors can support contractual rights requests (delete/access/correct/export) when required by customer agreements.

## 1) Data discovery
- Can the vendor locate an individual’s data across systems (including backups where relevant)?
- Search keys supported (email, ID, phone, account ID)?

## 2) Export / access support
- Can the vendor export data in a usable format?
- Time to produce export (SLA)?

## 3) Deletion support
- Can the vendor delete data across production systems?
- What is the deletion SLA?
- Does deletion propagate to subprocessors?
- How does vendor handle deletion in backups/logs?

## 4) Correction support
- Can the vendor correct/update data reliably?
- Audit log trail of changes available?

## 5) Retention alignment
- Vendor retention schedule documented?
- Can retention be configured per customer contract?

## 6) Verification & logging
- Evidence of DSAR handling (tickets/logs) available?
- Request tracking and proof of completion?

## 7) Subprocessors
- Does the vendor flow rights requests to subprocessors?
- Subprocessor response time expectations?

## 8) Contract inputs (must be explicit)
- DSAR support obligations (what vendor will do)
- SLA timelines
- Evidence/confirmation requirements
