# US Privacy Landscape Notes (Program Context)
US privacy is a **patchwork** of comprehensive state privacy laws with ongoing amendments and staged effective dates.
This TPRM program uses a **baseline privacy control set** and strengthens requirements for High/Critical vendors.

## What matters for TPRM
- vendor role obligations (processor/service provider style commitments)
- retention/deletion capability and proof
- subprocessor governance
- incident/breach coordination
