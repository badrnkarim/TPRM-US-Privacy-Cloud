# US Privacy Vendor Requirements (Baseline)
These requirements reflect common US privacy expectations across comprehensive state privacy laws and amendments.

## Required vendor commitments (baseline)
1) **Purpose limitation:** no processing beyond contracted purpose  
2) **Minimization:** only required data elements  
3) **Retention + deletion/return:** defined schedule + verifiable deletion at termination  
4) **Subprocessors:** disclosure + flow-down obligations + change notification  
5) **Access controls:** least privilege for personnel access to personal data  
6) **Incident/breach readiness:** IR plan + time-bound notification commitment  
7) **Rights support (contractual):** ability to support deletion/access/correction requests when required by contract  

## Evidence expectations
- Contract/DPA clause set (sanitized acceptable)
- Retention/deletion workflow and confirmation method
- Subprocessor list and change notification procedure
- IR plan summary and notification SLA
