# Approval Authority Matrix (Decision Makers by Tier)

This matrix defines **who must approve** a vendor decision based on tier.  
(Portfolio-grade; adjust to organization structure.)

## Approval roles
- **Business Owner:** accountable for business justification and scope
- **Security GRC (Owner):** accountable for risk rating, control requirements, evidence discipline
- **Privacy:** accountable for privacy obligations (purpose, retention, DSAR support, subprocessors)
- **Legal:** accountable for contract enforceability, DPA/security annex alignment
- **Procurement:** accountable for vendor onboarding/renewal controls
- **Risk Committee/Leadership:** required for Critical decisions and exceptions

## Tier-based authority
| Tier | Decision | Required Approvers | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Low | Approve | Business Owner + Security GRC | Standard terms acceptable |
| Medium | Approve/Conditional | Business Owner + Security GRC + Procurement | Evidence required; contract checklist applies |
| High | Approve/Conditional/Reject | Business Owner + Security GRC + Privacy + Legal + Procurement | US privacy & security annex reviewed |
| Critical | Conditional/Approve/Reject | **Risk Committee** (Security + Privacy + Legal + Leadership) + Business Owner | Privileged/VPN access or Restricted data requires leadership visibility |
| Any | Exception/Risk Acceptance | Security + Privacy + Legal + Business Owner + Risk Committee (if High/Critical) | Time-bound, documented, compensating controls mandatory |
