# Approval Authority Matrix
Low/Medium: Business Owner + Security GRC (+ Procurement for Medium)  
High: Business Owner + Security GRC + Privacy + Legal + Procurement  
Critical: Risk Committee/Leadership + Business Owner + Security GRC + Privacy + Legal  
Exceptions: time-bound, documented, compensating controls required.
