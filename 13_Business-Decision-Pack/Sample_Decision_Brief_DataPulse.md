# Executive Decision Brief — DataPulse Analytics (High Tier)

## Decision requested
✅ Approve with Conditions

## Business value
Analytics supports performance insights and segmentation for growth decisions.

## Data + access
- Data: Large-scale PII (Confidential/Restricted)
- Access: API + batch transfer (no VPN)
- Subprocessors: Yes (toolchain risk)

## Risk summary
- Inherent: 67 (High)
- Residual: ~15
- Key risks: minimization enforcement, retention/deletion proof, DSAR support SLAs, subprocessor notice window.

## Conditions
- C-06 Privacy annex: purpose limitation, DSAR SLAs, subprocessors flow-down + notice window, time-bound breach notification (EV-030/033/037)
- C-07 Minimization + retention/deletion proof: allowlist enforcement, retention schedule, deletion confirmation + subprocessor propagation (EV-031/032)

## Recommendation
Conditional approval is appropriate when privacy obligations are contractually enforceable and deletion/retention controls are proven.
