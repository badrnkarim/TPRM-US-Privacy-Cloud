# Inherent Risk Model (0–100)
Weights focus on: data sensitivity, access paths, criticality, privacy exposure, subprocessors, cloud complexity.
Tier thresholds:
0–24 Low | 25–44 Medium | 45–69 High | 70–100 Critical
