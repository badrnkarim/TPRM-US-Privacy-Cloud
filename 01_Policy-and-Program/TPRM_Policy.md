# TPRM Policy — US Privacy + Cloud Data Protection
**Owner:** Security GRC (Badr Karim) with Privacy + Legal oversight  
**Scope:** Vendors that store/process/transmit organizational data or receive system access  
**Objective:** Prevent unacceptable risk to confidentiality, integrity, availability, and privacy obligations.

## Tiers (based on inherent risk)
Low | Medium | High | Critical

## Approval authority
- Low/Medium: Business Owner + Security GRC
- High: Business Owner + Security GRC + Privacy + Legal + Procurement
- Critical: Risk Committee/Leadership + Business Owner + Security GRC + Privacy + Legal

## Minimum expectations
- Security safeguards: strong auth, encryption, logging, vuln mgmt
- Privacy safeguards: purpose limitation, minimization, retention/deletion, subprocessors, incident/breach coordination
