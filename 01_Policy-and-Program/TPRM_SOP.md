# TPRM SOP — Operational Steps

## Step 1: Intake
- Vendor owner submits: intake form + data classification + access needs
- Identify: data types, hosting model, access paths, subprocessors

## Step 2: Inherent risk scoring
- Score using `03_Risk-Scoring/Inherent_Risk_Model.md`
- Assign tier: Low/Medium/High/Critical

## Step 3: Due diligence
- Send tiered questionnaire
- Request evidence using `06_Evidence/Evidence_Acceptance_Criteria.md`

## Step 4: Review + residual risk
- Validate evidence quality and completeness
- Score residual risk using `03_Risk-Scoring/Residual_Risk_Model.md`

## Step 5: Decision
- Apply `07_Decision-Remediation/Decision_Matrix.md`
- If conditional: create remediation plan + due dates + owners

## Step 6: Contract controls
- Use `08_Contracts/US_Privacy_Cloud_Contract_Checklist.md`
- Confirm breach notification, subprocessors, deletion, and security obligations

## Step 7: Monitoring
- Apply ongoing monitoring playbook
- Trigger reassessment on major change, incident, or new data/access

## Step 8: Offboarding
- Remove access, confirm deletion/return, archive evidence, close record
