# Decision Matrix — Approve / Conditional / Reject

## Inputs
- Inherent risk score (0–100)
- Residual risk score (calculated)
- Tier (Low/Medium/High/Critical)
- Evidence confidence level (A/B/C)
- Unmitigated critical gaps (Y/N)

## Decisions
### Approve
- Residual risk is low
- No critical gaps
- Evidence confidence meets tier baseline

### Approve with conditions
- Residual risk moderate OR limited gaps
- Remediation plan required with deadlines
- Compensating controls documented

### Reject
- Residual risk high OR critical gaps unresolved
- Vendor cannot meet minimum controls or provide acceptable evidence
- Requires executive exception only if business-critical (rare)

## Mandatory escalations
- Critical tier always requires Risk Committee review
- Any vendor processing Restricted data with privileged access requires High/Critical baseline
