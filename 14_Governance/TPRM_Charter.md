# TPRM Charter
## Purpose
Establish authority and accountability for third-party risk decisions using risk-based controls and evidence.

## Scope
Vendors handling data or receiving access (SSO/API/admin/VPN/support tools), including subprocessors for High/Critical.

## Authority
Security GRC owns tiering and risk scoring; Privacy/Legal own contractual enforceability; Business owners own value justification; Risk Committee approves Critical and exceptions.

## Outputs
Policy/Standard/SOP, risk models, DDQ/evidence, decisions, remediation, monitoring, and audit trail.
