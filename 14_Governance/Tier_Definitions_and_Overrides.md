# Tier Definitions + Overrides
## Tier definitions
Low/Medium/High/Critical with examples of data/access drivers.

## Overrides (force higher tier)
- Restricted data
- Privileged/VPN access
- Critical business dependency
- Large-scale PII processing
- Untransparent subprocessors
