# Third-Party Risk Taxonomy
- Privacy risk (purpose/minimization/retention/DSAR)
- Security risk (auth, encryption, logging, VM, IR)
- Operational resilience (BC/DR, dependency)
- Compliance/legal risk (contract enforceability, subprocessors)
- Concentration risk (single point of failure)
- Fourth-party risk (toolchain/subprocessors)
