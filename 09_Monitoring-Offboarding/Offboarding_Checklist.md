# Offboarding Checklist (Vendor Exit)

- Disable accounts, API keys, tokens, VPN, and privileged access
- Confirm data return or deletion (obtain deletion certificate when possible)
- Confirm subprocessor deletion/return where applicable
- Archive evidence and decision records
- Close remediation items or transfer risk acceptance
- Update vendor inventory status to "Offboarded"
